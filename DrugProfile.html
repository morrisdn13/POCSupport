Amiodarone

(Cardarone)

250mg/500ml

Antiarrythmic

Increases the duration of ventricular and atrial muscle action by inhibiting Na,K-activated myocardial adenosine triphosphatase. There is a resulting decrease in heart rate and in vascular resistance
Uses and
Precautions		 Dosing Details

Uses

  • ventricular arrhythmias
  • atrial fibrillation
  • recurrent refractory ventricular fibrillation
  • ventricular tachycardia

Contraindications

  • Severe sinus node dysfunction
  • 2nd or 3rd degree AV block
  • Bradycardia with syncope unless paced
  • Cardiogenic shock

Precautions

  • Correct potassium, magnesium and calcium deficiencies prior to initiation
  • Increased risk of pulmonary toxicity or liver injury
  • Perform baseline pulmonary function tests at initiation
  • Do liver function tests regularly
  • Monitor thyroid function and signs of thyrotoxicosis
